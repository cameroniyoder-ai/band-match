🎵 Indianapolis Concert Match

Find out which upcoming Indianapolis-area concerts actually fit your music taste — paste your Spotify stats and every show gets ranked for you.

It's a single, self-contained web page. No app, no login, no install.

How to use it


Open the site.
Grab your stats from Stats for Spotify — log in with Spotify, then copy your Top Artists (and, for better results, your Top Genres).
Paste them into the two boxes and click Rank my shows.
Browse the ranked lineup — filter by venue or match tier, search, sort by match or date, hide tribute/cover bands, and click through to listen on Spotify or buy tickets.


Your stats are saved only in your own browser, so it remembers you next time. Use Edit my taste to update them.

How the matching works

Each show is tagged with its genre and a few comparable artists. The page compares those against the genres and artists you paste in, and gives every show a 0–100 match score:


Don't miss (90+) — an artist literally in your top list.
Strong (75–89) — squarely in a sound you love.
Worth a look (60–74) — overlaps your taste.
Maybe (40–59) — some overlap, off-center.
Skip (under 40) — not your lane.


All scoring runs in your browser. Pasting both artists and genres gives noticeably better results than artists alone. Scores are a helpful heuristic — great for "is this worth my time," not a Spotify algorithm.

Privacy

Nothing is uploaded. Your pasted stats stay in your browser's local storage and never leave your device.

Keeping it current

The concert lineup is baked into the page as a snapshot. To refresh it with newly announced shows, replace index.html with an updated version.
